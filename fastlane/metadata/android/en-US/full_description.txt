Advanced kernel management and system monitoring for Android. Features real-time performance overlay with customizable layouts, CPU/GPU frequency control, persistent swap management, and thermal monitoring. Material Design 3 UI with drag-and-drop reordering, sparkline graphs, and multi-stage warnings. Supports both root and Shizuku access. Monitor CPU, GPU, RAM, swap, power, and temperatures with adjustable update rates. Requires Android 7.0+.
